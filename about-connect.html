My Journey: From Classroom to Ascent Education Design

Why I Built Ascent Education Design

I left the classroom not because I didn't love teaching, but because I was watching trauma unfold in my students and my newly-fostered daughter - and had no idea how to help.

Traditional behavior management wasn't working. Even SEL curricula fell short. My daughter knew every calming strategy and emotion regulation tool we'd taught her. But when kindergarten overwhelmed her nervous system? Those tools became completely inaccessible.

Her brain was in survival mode - Watchdog or Possum state. And her teachers thought she was deliberately being defiant.

That's when I realized: SEL teaches the tools, but it doesn't teach what happens when kids can't access them.

Teachers were stuck. "I did everything I know, and it still isn't working. Now what?"

That "now what" became my mission.

The Vision

Ascent Education Design bridges the gap between SEL strategies and brain-state science, helping educators recognize when a student is dysregulated and providing co-regulation tools to bring that child back to a state where learning (and SEL tools) becomes possible again.

Because knowing what to do and being able to do it are two very different things.
